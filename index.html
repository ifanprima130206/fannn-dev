About me

A Full-Stack Web Developer Based in Purwakarta, Indonesia.

I am Ifan Prima Nursaid, born in Klaten on February 13, 2006, and currently 18 years old. I live in Purwakarta. My main hobby is coding, especially in creating websites. I have experience in using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, as well as various frameworks such as Bootstrap, Tailwind CSS, and also used Laravel and CodeIgniter for backend development. Currently, I am working at PT. Pratama Solusi Teknologi as a Web Developer. I am also studying at STT Watukancana, majoring in Informatics Engineering.

Portofolio

Gaspol

I developed GASPOL, an online platform for DPMPTSP Purwakarta to manage permits in health, housing, education, commerce, and spatial planning. The system simplifies processes, improves service accessibility, and speeds up approvals by digitizing and centralizing administrative tasks.

SICAKEP

SICAKEP is an application I developed for Bappelitbangda Purwakarta, designed to streamline the process for regional apparatus in Purwakarta to add plans that will be realized in their respective SIP applications.

Codeigniter 3 Bootstrap Jquery

SIP

SIP is an application I developed for the Regional Secretariat of Purwakarta, designed to implement planning from the SICAKEP website using a Restful API by the Purwakarta regional apparatus.

SIIMUT APIP

SIIMUT APIP is an application I developed for the Purwakarta Inspectorate, designed to simplify the creation of decision letters with multiple roles, including Person in Charge, Deputy Person in Charge, Report Evaluator, Technical Controller, Quality Controller, Team Leader, Team Member, and Regional Device Admin.

Laravel Bootstrap
Live

Mentari Bakery

Mentari Bakery is an online bakery platform that I developed as part of my first semester final project at STT Wastukancana's Informatics Engineering department. This platform allows customers to order bread online easily and conveniently through a website or application that I created.

E-Undangan, a work of mine, is a platform designed to cater to the needs of weddings. At its core, it allows for barcode check-in, the use of digital envelopes, payment through payment gateways, and the creation of a digital wedding gallery.

Wargabumi
